Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

CampusConnect is a platform designed to connect college students with alumni and recent graduates. It helps juniors seek guidance on placements, skills development, and career choices by accessing the real-world experiences of seniors.

CampusConnect is open to:

  • Current college students seeking mentorship or career guidance.
  • Recent graduates willing to mentor juniors and share experiences.
  • Faculty or campus placement officers interested in connecting students and alumni.

Go to our Sign Up page and choose the “Junior” option. Provide your name, email, and academic details. Once registered, you can browse senior profiles and reach out for guidance.

Sign up as a senior on our Sign Up page. Complete your profile by adding details like designation, company, graduation year, specializations, and the topics you’re willing to help with.
