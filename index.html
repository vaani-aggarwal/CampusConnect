Our Mission

Our motive is to connect juniors with placed seniors to bridge the gap between learning and achieving. We believe that real stories, honest advice, and shared experiences can empower students to prepare better for placements.
This platform is built to make mentorship easy, accessible, and meaningful—whether it's clearing doubts, finding resources, or just hearing how someone else did it.
